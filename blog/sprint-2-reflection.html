Add HTML and CSS to your Blog Challenge

Write a small story about HTML and CSS and their relationship. You could personify elements or speak from your own personal experience. The story should capture insight about the nature of HTML and CSS.

Meet HTML and CSS. They work together to make books! It was not always like this though. Before they met eachother, HTML was an author and a good one at that. However, nobody ever read his great stories because the cover was ugly and there were no pictures. CSS was also an author, he made amazing looking covers and had beautiful pictures to accompany his writing. The only problem was that his stories were boring and nobody finished reading his books. One fateful day when they both visited their favorite bookstore "Chromebooks" they bumped into eachother. HTML noticed how beautiful CSS's book covers looked and asked him "did you make those?". HTML had an amazing idea that the two should linkup and work together! From that day on, every book that HTML wrote was accompanied by the beautiful covers and pictures of CSS.

What obstacles did you come up against? How did you overcome them?

The main obstacle for me was getting the position of elements right in CSS. I wanted to try and apply a bit of everything that I learnt in FreeCodeCamp without overcrowding the blog page. But to keep it clean I needed to correctly position everything. It came down to a bit more research as to how to position elements in the way that I wanted. I figure that this will be a regular approach because every layout is different and will have its own problems.

Any moments of surprise or joy?
